Settings & Audit gives administrators controlled configuration surfaces and traceability for important mutations. This feature depends on the P4.2 permission baseline so settings and audit access can be enforced consistently.

This SRS is intentionally a placeholder until Feature 3 starts. BA confirmation is required before filling implementation rules.
