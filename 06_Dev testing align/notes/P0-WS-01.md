# P0-WS-01 — Workspace identity and access

## Audit scope

- Environments: DevInt `https://rally-dev.qnsc.vn/` and local mockup `http://127.0.0.1:5173/`
- Account: `hieuvbm@qnsc.vn`
- Audit date: 2026-07-19
- Context: `NXP / All Teams`
- Routes/areas: header context selector and `/settings`
- Mutation level: read-only; no settings were saved

## Confirmed baseline

- The MVP serves one fixed Workspace.
- Users cannot create, switch, archive or delete Workspaces.
- Project and Team are subordinate selectable contexts.
- The visible and internal product term is `Workspace`.
- Workspace Admin may edit Workspace name.

## DevInt observations

- Header resolves one fixed root: `ACME Corp`.
- No workspace list, create, archive or switch action is exposed.
- Project and Team selection remain below the fixed root.
- Teams is correctly located under the Settings gear.
- The selector labels the root as `Organization`.
- Settings groups the same concept under `Workspace` and exposes `Workspace Settings`, `Workspace admin` and `Workspace name`.
- `Workspace name` is an enabled required textbox containing `ACME Corp`, with a `Save changes` button.

## Mockup observations

- Header root is `ACME Space Inc.` and the selector labels it `Company / Workspace`.
- Settings uses `Workspace Settings` and `Workspace Admin` terminology.
- Teams is correctly located under the Settings gear.
- Clicking the sidebar Workspace Settings entry does not replace the Profile & Account content, so there is no working mockup form to confirm editability.

## Match and gaps

### Match

- One fixed tenant identity is clear.
- No self-service Workspace CRUD/switch is present.
- Company → Project → Team hierarchy is preserved.
- Teams placement matches the confirmed Settings navigation decision.

### GAP-P0-WS-001 — terminology

SRS, mockup and DevInt mix `Company`, `Organization` and `Workspace` for the same visible object. BA confirmed `Workspace` is authoritative. DevInt must replace the selector label `Organization`; SRS and mockup must replace remaining `Company` and `Company / Workspace` wording.

### GAP-P0-WS-002 — display-name editability

DevInt allows the fixed Workspace name to be edited. BA confirmed this is correct. The SRS and mockup must be updated with Workspace Admin authorization, required-name validation, save feedback and audit-event acceptance criteria. No mutation was executed during this audit.

## BA confirmation

Confirmed on 2026-07-19:

1. `ACME Corp` versus `ACME Space Inc.` is environment/configuration data, not a business gap, provided each environment resolves exactly one fixed Workspace.
2. Teams remains under Settings.
3. Standardize all user-facing terminology to `Workspace`.
4. Workspace Admin may edit Workspace name.
